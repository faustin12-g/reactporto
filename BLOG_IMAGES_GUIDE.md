## Design Recommendations

### Visual Style
- Maintain consistency with your portfolio aesthetic
- Use your brand colors: Cyan (#00d4ff) and Pink (#ff6b9d)
- Keep a professional, modern look
- Ensure good contrast for readability

### Technical Optimization
- Export as PNG (better quality) or optimized JPG
- Resize to 800x600px or similar 4:3 ratio
- Compress images (use TinyPNG or similar tools)
- Target file size: 200-400KB each

### Color Scheme
- Primary: #00d4ff (Cyan)
- Accent: #ff6b9d (Pink)
- Dark Background: #0f0f1e
- Text: #a8a8b3 (Gray)
